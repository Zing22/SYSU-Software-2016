Sign up and Log in

If you have registered CRAFT before, click LOG IN tab on main page to sign in.
If you are a new user of CRAFT, register a new CARFT account on main page craft.sysusoftware. info with your e-mail address and then you can use CRAFT Designer to design your own biological system and share it with other researchers on CRAFT Square.
Also you can scan other designs on CRAFT Square in guest mode, but CRAFT Designer is unavailable in this mode.

Profile page

In this page, all your designs are listed, including your marked designs.

Square page

You can view all open designs in this page, and “like” them or “mark” them. If you find someone try to use CRAFT design systems that have safety problems, you can report them on Square.
We will receive your report and deal with it.

Search Box on top menu

The search box provides you with related users and open designs.

Design details

Open the design detail page by clicking design name on Square page or Profile page, where you can check every single step of a design.

If it is your design, you can modify its design details, share or withdraw your share, and even delete your design.

Settings

You can enter the Settings page through the drop-down bar from top menu. In this page you can change your photo, nickname or passcode.

Other's profile

You can click other users' nicknames to enter their profiles on Square or profile page, and visit their designs and marked designs.

Feedback

You can give us feedback with e-mail by clicking the mail-box-like botton on the right side of any page.

What can CRAFT do?

It can automatically design biological systems and generate supporting experiments at the same time. Also, by using CRAFT, users can share their own protocols designed by our software with others online and help them solve their problems.

How should I use CRAFT's automatically designed function?

Enter CRAFT Designer, upload your constraints or enter them manually. Our software will automatically compute and generate designs together with experiment protocols.

What else we should pay attention when using CRAFT?

Obey local laws and regulations and strictly follow standard experiment procedures.

Why CRAFT use distributed computing?

To satisfy the huge demand for computing work.

Do CRAFT have any restrictions for data usage and spread?

We don't impose any additional constraints on data. However, owing to the regulations from databases, CRAFT's data could only be used by individual users. Please do not change data or distribute them outside CRAFT.

What should I do if CRAFT fails to find my targets?

Please check if your targets are labeled with “toxic” in China Catalog of Hazardous Chemicals first, because these chemicals with this label had been deleted in CRAFT. If you confirm all your targets are safe, please contact with us and provide related messages of your targets including reactions and chassis species, then we will add your targets into our database.
