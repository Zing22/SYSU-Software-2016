Remove cells from -80 freezer and thaw in your hand.
Add 1-5 l containing 1 pg-100 ng of plasmid DNA to the cell mixture. Carefully flick the tube 4-5 times to mix cells and DNA. Do not vortex.
Place the mixture on ice for 2 minutes. Do not mix.
Heat shock at exactly 42 for exactly 30 seconds. Do not mix.
Place on ice for 2 minutes. Do not mix.
Pipette 950 l of room temperature SOC into the mixture. Immediately spread 50-100 l onto a selection plate and incubate overnight at 37-42.
